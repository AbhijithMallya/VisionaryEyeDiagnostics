OPTI  SCAN
EYE DISEASE DETECTOR

A software to detect test and classify eye disease like cataract glaucoma and diabetic retinopathy in human eyes.

Categorization of SAMPLES

Sorting eyes into four, a vision project to explore. Cataract, retinopathy, glaucoma, or clear, our pixels unveil what's near.

Cataract

 A clouding of the eye's lens, causing blurred vision and, if untreated, leading to visual impairment.

Diabetic Retinopathy

A diabetes-related condition affecting the retina, potentially causing vision loss due to damaged blood vessels.

Glaucoma

Causes optic nerve damage, associated with increased intraocular pressure, and leading to progressive vision loss.

Normal

Healthy eyes without significant abnormalities or diseases, providing a baseline for categorization process.

Demo

Selet an image of the format jpg / jpeg / png and upload it. The Uploaded image is classified into one of the 4 categories mentioned above.
